### Professional — $29/mo

### Enterprise — $99/seat/mo

## What You're Paying For

The software is open source and free. Membership pays for:
- **Cloud infrastructure** — sync, updates, analytics
- **Support** — humans who know marine systems
- **Training** — technician network access
- **Convenience** — preloaded hardware that just works

You can always fork everything and run it yourself. The hardware is standard Jetson/RPi. The moat isn't lock-in.

## Billing

- Monthly or annual (2 months free on annual)
- Pay via Stripe on Cocapn.com
- Hardware purchases separate at Deckboss.net
- No hidden fees, no usage charges
