# cocapn-site

> Cocapn.com — Open source agent infrastructure. Pay only for convenience.

**Live:** [cocapn-site.casey-digennaro.workers.dev](https://cocapn-site.casey-digennaro.workers.dev)

## Membership

| Tier | Price | Key Benefits |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | 5 designs/day, 1 instance, community support |
| Standard | $9/mo | Unlimited designs, 5 instances, cloud sync, email support |
| Professional | $29/mo | Unlimited, Capitaine.ai, priority support, white-label |
| Enterprise | $99/seat | Dedicated support, custom hardware, SLA, on-site training |

## Why Pay?

- **Cost-plus pricing** that approaches zero for loyal high-performers
- **Fork and self-host** — everything is MIT licensed
- **Token economy** — shared content is free, fresh costs tokens, remix is cheap
- **Cloud services** — sync, updates, analytics, monitoring

## Company

Built in Alaska by commercial fishermen and marine technicians who needed better tools. The same AI that monitors fishing vessels now powers robotics systems, content pipelines, and agent networks worldwide.

Open source is not our business model. It is our conviction.
